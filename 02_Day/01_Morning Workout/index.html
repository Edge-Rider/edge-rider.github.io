Welcome to Rome!

WelcomeToRome is a fascinating and surprising show dedicated to Rome. You can live there a unique experience: to dive into 2700 years of history of the Eternal City. Through an exciting “time machine” you will be able to go through the fundamental stages that have brought Rome to become the city we know today.
